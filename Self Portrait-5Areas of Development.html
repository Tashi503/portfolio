Emotional Development

I thought spiritual development is the same as religion. Now I know it's different. Religion is the belief we have and spiritual is being the best that we can be in anything. Being a good human. It made me become a better human being and it made me never give up but give my 100% in anything I do.

Till I joined The Royal Academy, I never played Basketball, volley or outdoor sports, but now I play and I am in the School team of football and basketball. Now I play every sport. My strength and flexibility have improved a lot because of morning physical and programs at The Royal Academy. I have learned many basic health treatments like CPR, RICE and more. I have learned many new indigenous games and I enjoyed playing them.
I was very poor in controlling my emotions. Like I used to feel angry easily and show out my anger, or when I get sad I take a lot of time to recover from it. I don't know what to do. I don't know how to balance my emotions, but Emotional Development helped me to control my emotions and balance my emotions. When I feel some negative emotions I try to find the cause and try to solve it and try not to repeat that cause again. I try to use positive emotions. When I become emotional I play guitar or sports to control my emotions. Now when I feel something I realize what I am feeling and how to control it not fully but some.
